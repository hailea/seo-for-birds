Google’s John Mueller gives one of the best reasons why you shouldn’t go with multiple domain names for each business or location:
"Focusing on a single website makes it much easier for our algorithms to understand your site, the services you’re providing, and the regions that you service. Splitting that into separate sites for each location or business that you ship to not only makes it harder for our algorithms, but also for users (imagine if the post office did that).” You can imagine how confusing and hard it would be to find what you need if the post office had a website for each branch."

Readability
Medtroniuc website are not simplified for blind users

Techniques used to improve readibility of the site for blind users are:

    Source: American foundation for the blind
  1. Relative font sizes: Most fonts are relative but not all
  2. Provide alternative texts for images
  3. Name Links carefully: Never use click here or learn more
  4. Explicitly State information
  5. Provide Skip links: - Skip links allow the speech software or braille user to bypass information that is repeated on every page, such as navigation bars.
    6. Skip Main Navigation
