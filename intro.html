Search Engine Optimization

the process of maximizing the number of visitors to a particular website by ensuring that the site appears high on the list of results returned by a search engine
  1. The main purpose of search engine optimization is to improve sites interaction with both users and search engines
  2. Visitors of the site are the main consumers of the content and search engines are used to find the content
  3. Google, Ping and Yahoo are soome of the most common serach engines
  4. Serach engine Optimization affects only organic search results, not paid or sponsored results such as Google Adwords
  5. Search Engine Optimization is a type of digital marketing. Its purpose is to make the website upfront in different search engine results.
